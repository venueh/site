Plans and Approvals

Every commercial construction project in Tamil Nadu requires DTCP approval, building plan sanction from the Coimbatore Municipal Corporation or DTCP, and TNRERA registration where applicable. Venue Housing manages this end-to-end — plan preparation, submission, liaison with government authorities, and tracking through to sanction. We know the local process, the current timelines, and the right channels.
